Supporting Projects for Adolescents


Bangladesh around 23% of the total populations are adolescent which constitute almost 40 millions are suffering from lack of access to accurate information, friendly health services with social stigma and inadequate enabling environment that makes the situation complex. To promote adolescents girls’ and boys’ physical and mental health, SKNF addresses reproductive health and sexual behavior, malnutrition, early marriage, gender discrimination, healthy life style and finally their empowerment.

Supporting Scholarship Programs


Adolescents are the future productive force, parents and caregivers. They are the future innovators, leaders and the change makers whose education is not ensured properly. Thousand of talented adolescents are leaving school due to financial inability and other causes. SKNF is offering scholarship for talented but needy boys and girls for their future education through mobilizing local resources. Scholarships are provided on monthly basis through bank account to bear the educational expenses.

Creative Donation


As a registered non-profit and social change maker development organization in Bangladesh, SKNF is interested and qualified enough to receive local and international donation to run the projects and programs. Donations are used for development activities with clear financial policies and guideline given by the donors as well as by regulators. SKNF is responsible to use your support properly and believe your donation may save millions of lives in needed.

Partnership in Programming


SKNF believes in collaboration and partnership in its projects and programmes. It has proven experiences to work in partnership with Government, UN agencies, NGOs, INGOs, private sectors and media. In order to mobilize policy makers, politician, high officials, community leaders, opinion leaders, parents, local leaders and local administrators for policy issues SKNF developed network and communication with mutual respects and interest. We are open to build partnership under mutual understanding as it is the core value of SNK.
